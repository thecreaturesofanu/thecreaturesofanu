# Initial General Meeting

The Creatures of ANU Inaugural General Meeting 

Hancock west level 3 

29/03/2026

Minutes by Liam Cook u7875901

**5:45pm**
Meeting commenced at 5:45pm. Names and UID of attendees recorded.

**5:50pm**
Technical difficulties regarding online attendees resolved.

Copies of the constitution are distributed.

Acknowledgement of country done by Maya. President candidates (Hayley and Maya) read through the constitution aloud. 

Clarification of membership fee as ‘optional $5’. Not everyone has to contribute the optional $5 so it is discussed being removed from the constitution. 

**6:10pm**

Motion to strike 5.2 and 5.3 from the constitution (optional subscription fee)
-	Hayley: moves motion
-	Tyler: seconds motion 
-	Nobody speaks against the motion. Unanimous decision to ratify the motion.

Clarification on rule to keep the student name and ID of members.

**6:15pm**

Discussion if non-anu students can become members as per the constitution. Student members can vote in elections, and there are no provisions in the constitution for non-student members.

Motion to replace instances of ‘full member’ with ‘member’ and ‘full membership’ and ‘membership’ in the constitution for clarification purposes.
-	Maya: moves motion
-	Abigail: seconds motion
-	Discussion on if ANUSA would require ‘full member’ terminology, but consensus seems to be that it is unnecessary. 
-	Votes for replacing the terminology is unanimously positive.

**6:20pm**

Motion to accept constitution with amendments
-	Avery: moves motion
-	Abigail: seconds motion
-	Voting for accepting the constitution is unanimous, and the constitution is formally accepted.

**6:25pm**

Returning officer: Alexander

Nominations for executive positions are called, with opportunities for in person and online attendees to nominate.

Treasurer nominations: Callum

Secretary nominations: Liam, Abigail

President nominations: Maya, Hayley

No further nominations for president or secretary.

**6:30pm**

Each speech is to be limited by 3 minutes.

Maya is first to speak for their president nomination. Hayley is the second to speak for their president nomination.

Abigail is first to speak for their secretary nomination. Liam is the second to speak for their secretary nomination.

Callum is the only nomination for the treasurer, but still speaks for their nomination.

Voting is open from 6:45 to 6:55, allowing time for in person and online votes. The voting is conducted via a form, with each person given a voting code.

**7pm**

Votes counted and announced by Alexander.

President winner: Maya

Secretary winner: Liam

Treasurer winner: Callum
Motion to accept the results of the election and allow the elected executives to be trustees of the club bank account.
-	Avery: moves motion
-	Abigail: Seconds the motion to accept the results of the election and let the elected executives be trustees of the club bank account.
-	Votes to accept the motion are unanimously positive, and the executive team is officially chosen.
Executive team declares the general meeting to close.

**7:02pm end**
